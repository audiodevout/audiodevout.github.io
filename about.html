About

Atharva Gupta is a new media artist, sculptor, and researcher who builds objects, systems, and situations grounded in personal relationships with technology. His practice moves through sound art, kinetic sculpture, audiovisual performance, and experimental media, treating technology as something emotional, erratic, and entangled with both body and politics.
