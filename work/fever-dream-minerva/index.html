Fever Dream

AV Performance

May 2025 · Minerva Academy · Groningen, NL

Experimental A/V night at Minerva. First public showing of the Gesture-Controlled Performance System (G.C.P.S.S.M.—Gesture-Controlled Performance System for Slow Music): performer movement drives live sound and image—body and patch read as one environment.
