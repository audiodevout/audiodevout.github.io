Spaces @ OOST

VJ Performance

31 August 2024 · OOST · Groningen, NL

Reimagining the arts, the multisensory way. During our Season Opening this Saturday art collective Spaces gets carte blanche in our upstairs ambient room. In short time and through exhibitions, readings, music events, performances and more, the dedicated crew has fostered an even more dedicated circle of collaborators, friends and followers. All of these meet between 01:00 and 05:00 for a brain tingling cross-pollination of music, poetry and light installations. Taking five has never felt this good. VJ work at Ambient Room: live ambient sets, poetry, and image in the same room—sound, text, and projection kept deliberately slow and sensory.
