'Virtual' Bodies in Sonic Space

Production & Technical Assistance

18–21 August 2023 · VRIJDAG Theater · Groningen, NL

VR theater by Chantalla Pleiter & Jan Klug at Noorderzon (local program). Production and technical assistance. With a VR headset on your head, your reality suddenly becomes very different—the world takes shape based on what you see. Go on an exploration with others and build your own surprising and magical dimension.
