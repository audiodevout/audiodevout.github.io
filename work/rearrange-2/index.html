RE:Arrange! #2

Group Exhibition (FMI MADtech)

17–18 April 2026 · NP3 / RE:Search:Gallery · Groningen, NL

Second version of RE:Arrange! at NP3 RE:Search:Gallery, RE:Arranged by Sander Jorn Vermeulen (Kunstpunt Groningen — Art in Public Space). NP3 × Frank Mohr Institute collaboration bringing Painting and MADtech work into dialogue through a public-space curatorial lens. Instruments for Becoming shown as a motor-driven kinetic installation.
