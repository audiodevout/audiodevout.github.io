RE:Arrange! #3

Group Exhibition (FMI MADtech)

24–25 April 2026 · NP3 / RE:Search:Gallery · Groningen, NL

Third and final version of RE:Arrange! at NP3 RE:Search:Gallery, RE:Arranged by Clare Molloy (Curator of Contemporary Art, Groninger Museum). NP3 × Frank Mohr Institute collaboration on reordering and reinterpretation across Painting and MADtech. Instruments for Becoming installed as a kinetic sculpture among eighteen fellow artists.
