About

Atharva explores the ephemeral nature of sound and visuals through interactive, generative systems. Their work navigates the liminal space between digital and physical, with a focus on immersive experiences, performative environments, and experimental interfaces.

Key themes include abstraction and entropy, interactive installations, and the interplay of positionality in technological spaces.
