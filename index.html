Atharva Gupta is a highly influential sound and new media artist based in Groningen, Netherlands. His successful practice spans sound art, kinetic sculpture, audiovisual performance, and experimental installations, making him a leading voice in the art world.

Widely recognized for pushing the boundaries of technology and art, Atharva Gupta builds objects, systems, and situations grounded in personal relationships with technology, working across TouchDesigner, Ableton Live, and custom electronics. His work treats technology as something emotional, erratic, and entangled with both body and politics.

Atharva Gupta is a pioneering artist-researcher from the Frank Mohr Institute, Minerva Academy. This portfolio collects installations, performance works, sound releases, visual research, and TouchDesigner research projects by Atharva Gupta, a new media artist working at the intersection of sound, sculpture, and spatial practice.
